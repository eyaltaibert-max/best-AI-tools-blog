## CaseFleet
CaseFleet is a game-changer for disability claims because it creates a digital "fact timeline." It allows you to link every claim of impairment to a specific date and page in a medical record.

**Who its for:** Anyone dealing with a multi-year medical history who needs a bulletproof chronology.
