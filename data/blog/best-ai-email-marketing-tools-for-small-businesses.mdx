## Mailchimp — Best for Beginners

Mailchimp is the name most small business owners hear first — and for good reason. Its drag-and-drop editor now includes AI-powered content generation, so you can go from blank page to polished email in minutes. **Who it's for:** Brick-and-mortar shops, solo consultants, and early-stage startups that need to look professional without hiring a copywriter.

**Pricing:** Free tier available; paid plans start at $20/month. Note: costs climb steeply as your list grows, and the best AI features are gated behind higher tiers.

## FAQ

### Do I need technical skills to use AI email marketing tools?

No technical skills are required for most AI email marketing tools. Platforms like Mailchimp and SendX offer drag-and-drop editors and AI-generated content suggestions that make it easy for beginners to create professional campaigns without coding knowledge.

### Which AI email marketing tool is best for a Shopify store?

Klaviyo is widely considered the best AI email marketing tool for Shopify stores. It offers deep native Shopify integration, AI-powered predictive analytics for customer lifetime value and churn risk, and built-in revenue tracking to directly attribute sales to your email campaigns.

### Can I switch email marketing tools without losing my subscriber list?

Yes, you can switch email marketing platforms without losing your subscriber list. Most tools allow you to export your contacts as a CSV file, which you can then import into your new platform. It is best practice to re-confirm subscriber consent after migrating to maintain deliverability and comply with email regulations.
