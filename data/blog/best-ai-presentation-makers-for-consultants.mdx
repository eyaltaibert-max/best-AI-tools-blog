## FAQ

**Q: Can these AI presentation tools match the quality of a professional designer?**
For speed and first drafts, yes — tools like Gamma and Beautiful.ai produce results that would have required a junior designer a half-day to produce. For highly bespoke, pixel-perfect brand work, a human designer still wins. The practical reality for most consulting engagements is that an AI-generated deck that is 85% of the way there and delivered in 20 minutes beats a perfect deck delivered after the meeting.

**Q: Which AI presentation tool is best for teams rather than individual consultants?**
Beautiful.ai is the strongest choice for consulting teams because of its centralized brand kit and template management. Canva AI also has strong team collaboration features and is worth considering if your team needs a broader design toolkit beyond presentations.

**Q: Do these tools export to PowerPoint format for clients who require it?**
Gamma, Beautiful.ai, and Canva AI all support PowerPoint export. Tome is a web-native platform with more limited traditional export options. SlidesAI outputs natively to Google Slides, which can then be exported to PowerPoint. Always verify export compatibility with your specific client requirements before committing to a tool.
