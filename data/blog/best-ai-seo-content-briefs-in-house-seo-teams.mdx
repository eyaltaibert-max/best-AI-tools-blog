## FAQ

**What is an AI SEO content brief?**

An AI SEO content brief is an automatically generated document that outlines target keywords, recommended structure, word count, and competitor insights to guide content creation.

**How much do AI content brief tools cost?**

Prices range from $15/month for entry-level tools like Frase to $189/month for premium platforms like Clearscope.

**Can AI content briefs replace manual SEO research?**

AI briefs significantly reduce research time but work best when combined with human editorial judgment.
