## How to Choose the Right AI Chatbot Builder

When selecting an AI chatbot for your e-commerce store, consider these factors:

1. **Platform compatibility** — Make sure the tool integrates with your e-commerce platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)
2. **AI capabilities** — Look for natural language processing that can handle real customer queries
3. **Automation features** — Abandoned cart recovery, order tracking, and product recommendations are must-haves
4. **Pricing** — Start with a free plan if available, then scale as your store grows
5. **Support quality** — The best chatbot should reduce support tickets, not create more

## FAQ

### What is the best AI chatbot for Shopify stores?

Tidio is widely considered the best AI chatbot for Shopify stores. It offers a native Shopify integration, live chat, and AI-powered automation that can recover abandoned carts and handle customer queries 24/7.

### Are there free AI chatbot builders for e-commerce?

Yes. Tidio and ManyChat both offer free plans suitable for small e-commerce stores. These free tiers include basic chatbot flows and limited monthly conversations to get you started without upfront cost.

### How do AI chatbots help increase e-commerce sales?

AI chatbots increase e-commerce sales by recovering abandoned carts, providing instant product recommendations, answering pre-purchase questions around the clock, and reducing friction in the buying journey — all of which improve conversion rates.
