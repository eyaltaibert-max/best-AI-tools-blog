## FAQ

**Q: Do I need a vector file (SVG) for my startup logo?**
Yes. Vector files scale without quality loss and are required for print materials, signage, and high-resolution digital use. Always make sure your chosen plan includes SVG.

**Q: Will an AI-generated logo be unique to my startup?**
Most AI logo generators create unique combinations based on your inputs, but some elements may overlap with other users. Always customize the output and consider trademark registration.
