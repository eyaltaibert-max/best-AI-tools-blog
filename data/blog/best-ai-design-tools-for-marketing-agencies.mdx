## Figma AI

## Canva Magic Studio

**AI-powered design platform for creating marketing visuals at scale**

Canva democratized design long before AI arrived, and Magic Studio deepens that advantage. Magic Design generates complete, branded layouts from a text prompt in seconds. The Brand Kit locks in your logo, color palette, and fonts so every output stays on-brand without manual configuration. **Who it's for:** Marketing teams with non-designer staff who need consistent, on-brand content produced quickly and cheaply.

## Midjourney

No other tool on this list comes close to Midjourney's image quality. ## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which AI design tool is best for a marketing agency without in-house designers?**
Canva Magic Studio is the clear choice. Its drag-and-drop interface, massive template library, and Brand Kit mean anyone on your team can produce on-brand content without design training. The AI features accelerate output without requiring any technical knowledge.

**Are AI-generated design assets safe to use commercially for client work?**
It depends on the tool. Adobe Firefly is the safest option — it was trained on licensed content and Adobe explicitly indemnifies commercial use. Midjourney permits commercial use on paid plans, but the training data situation is less clear-cut. Always review each platform's commercial use policy before delivering AI-generated assets to clients.

**Can AI design tools replace a professional designer at a marketing agency?**
Not yet — and not for premium work. AI tools are at their best when handling volume, speed, and iteration: generating multiple ad variants, creating social content at scale, or roughing out prototypes. For brand identity work, complex campaigns, or any deliverable where originality is the product, a professional designer's judgment remains essential.
