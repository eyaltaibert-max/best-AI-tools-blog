For 20 years, the "Google it" reflex has dominated the internet. But Perplexity AI is introducing a new reflex: "Ask it." The difference is fundamental—Google gives you a list of places to find an answer; Perplexity gives you the answer itself.

## When to use Google Search
Google is still king when you need a **destination**, not an answer.
- Finding a specific login page.
- Searching for a local business (Maps integration).
- Browsing a specific forum (like Reddit) to see real human opinions.

## When to use Perplexity AI
Perplexity wins when you need **information synthesis**.
- "What are the three best ways to reduce CAC for a B2B SaaS in 2026?"
- "Compare the tax implications of an LLC vs S-Corp in California."
- "Summarize the latest news on the GPT-5 release rumors."

## The Verdict: The Hybrid Workflow
The most productive users are using a hybrid approach. They use Perplexity for the "First Pass" of research to understand the landscape, and Google for the "Final Pass" to find specific official documents or local services.

**The Winner:** For knowledge work, **Perplexity AI** is the clear productivity winner.
