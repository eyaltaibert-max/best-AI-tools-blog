# Best AI Meeting Note Takers for Sales Teams in 2026

## What Are AI Meeting Note Takers for Sales Teams?

AI meeting note takers automatically record, transcribe, and summarize sales calls — then push key details directly into your CRM. For sales teams juggling dozens of client calls weekly, these tools eliminate manual note-taking and ensure no deal detail slips through the cracks.

## How We Evaluated These Tools

We tested each platform on CRM integration depth, recording flexibility (bot-free vs. bot-joined), sales framework support, pricing transparency, and real-user ratings from G2 and Capterra.
