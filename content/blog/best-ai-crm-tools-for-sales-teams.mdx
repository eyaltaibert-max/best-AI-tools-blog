## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the difference between AI CRM lead scoring and AI agents?**

Lead scoring uses machine learning to rank existing leads by conversion probability. AI agents (like Agentforce or Attio research agents) are autonomous — they take actions, update records, send follow-ups, and enrich data without being prompted. For most SMB teams, lead scoring delivers the fastest ROI. Agents require more mature data infrastructure.

**Q: Can I use AI CRM tools without a large existing data set?**

AI features work best with historical data, but modern platforms like HubSpot and Pipedrive start delivering value early. Breeze AI and Pipedrive AI Sales Assistant draw on aggregated benchmarks while your own data accumulates. Attio AI enrichment actively builds your dataset from external sources.

**Q: Is Salesforce worth the cost for small sales teams?**

Rarely. The Starter plan is accessible at $25/user/month, but the AI features that make Salesforce compelling unlock at Enterprise pricing ($175+/user/month) — and you will need admin capacity. For teams under 50 reps, HubSpot or Pipedrive will deliver more value per dollar.
