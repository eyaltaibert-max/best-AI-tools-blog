## FAQ

### What is the best AI user testing tool for small product teams?
Hotjar offers a generous free tier with heatmaps, session recordings, and AI-powered survey analysis, making it the most accessible option for small teams.

### Can AI user testing tools recruit participants for me?
Yes — Maze, UserTesting, and Lyssna all offer built-in participant panels, while Hotjar and Sprig rely on your existing site or app traffic.

### What's the difference between usability testing and behavior analytics?
Usability testing tools like Maze and UserTesting run structured tests with specific tasks, while behavior analytics tools like Hotjar observe how users naturally interact with your live product.
