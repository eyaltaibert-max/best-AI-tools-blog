## FAQ

**Q: Can AI legal research tools replace a paralegal?**
Not entirely. Tools like Lexis+ AI and CoCounsel dramatically reduce research time, but they work best when a trained legal professional reviews and validates the output. Think of them as a first-pass research assistant, not a replacement for legal judgment.

**Q: Are AI-generated legal citations reliable?**
It depends on the tool. CoCounsel and Lexis+ AI are specifically designed to tie AI responses to verifiable sources from their respective databases. Generic AI tools like ChatGPT are not suitable for legal citation work due to hallucination risk.

**Q: Which AI legal research tool is best for solo practitioners?**
Lexis+ AI offers the most flexibility across firm sizes and has a reputation for being accessible to solo and small-firm lawyers, not just enterprise clients. It's the best starting point if you're a solo practitioner exploring AI research tools.
